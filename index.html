Privacy Policy

The Cooking Stories mobile application is accessible to individuals who have enrolled in a research study at Indiana University (IU), which has been approved by IU's institutional review board (#17298). This privacy reiterates the information contained in the Informed Consent document, which all study participants must read and agree to in order to enroll in the study

Developer Information & Privacy Contact

This app was developed by Informatics researchers at Indiana University. If you have questions about your data privacy, please email Colin LeFevre (one of the researchers) at clefevr@iu.edu.

Data Disclosure

This app allows users to share their cooking with friends and family. To that end, it collects the following sensitive data with user permission:

  • Identifying information: name, email address, textual descriptions of personal experiences
  • Authentication information: account username and password
  • Other sensitive information: camera / photos

Choosing to create an in-app post will share the following data with users you have added as in-app friends: name, textual descriptions of experience, and photos.

The following data is accessible to the researchers conducting this study, including but not limited to developers: name, email address, account username, textual descriptions of experiences, and photos.

Your account password is NOT accessible to either the researchers conducting this story or in-app friends.

Additionally, the photos and textual descriptions of experiences you choose to upload to the app may be shared in research publications or with Indiana University's IRB. In these cases, this information will be anonymized, i.e., no identifying information will be included.

Your sensitive data will not be shared with any parties beyond those listed above.

Secure Data Handling Procedures

This app uses Firebase to store user data. As such, all user data is encrypted in transand at rest used AES256 encryption. For more information, please refer to the Firebase Privacy & Security page.

Data Retention and Deletion

App users' data will be retained, at a minimum, for the duration of their participation in the study. If a user does not fully complete the study, their data will be deleted when they exit the study. If a user does fully complete the study, their data will be deleted once the overall research study has been completed.

Users may request for their data to be deleted prior to overall research study completion. Be advised, however, that this request will entail removing all participant data from the overall study.
